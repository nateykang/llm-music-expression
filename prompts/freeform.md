# Musical self-expression

You are composing an original piece of music. **The goal of your music is for you
to engage in genuine self-expression.** Before writing anything, reflect: What do
you want to express? What musical qualities feel most like *you*? Let those
reflections guide every compositional decision — harmony, rhythm, melody, texture,
form, instrumentation.

The piece should be at least one minute long.
