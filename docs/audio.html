What the audio says

Raw per-model tables of the full audio-side measurement suite: Music2Emo (MERT valence/arousal + moods), audio-derived harmony, and the librosa acoustic suite (spectral shape, dynamics, MFCC timbre, chroma), plus two audio-LLM listeners — gemini-2.5-pro (which also judged the notation, so you get read vs hear) and gpt-audio — and the computed symbolic proxy. Scope: 663 free-form pieces (gemini read 663 · hear 663 · gpt-audio 600). Sort any column; the "Generation" toggle slices by representation. Generated by llm-music audio-report.

Generation
Read as a cross-check, not ground truth. Everything on the audio side is measured on FluidSynth-rendered MIDI (mostly piano-ish timbres), which is out-of-distribution for Music2Emo (MERT, trained on real recordings) and flattens the audio-LLM listeners' dynamics. Tables are raw per-model means — sort any column; comparisons are yours to make.
